To control the simulated game on Nokia LCD using the Tiva Board:

1. Open VirtualNokia5110.c file.

2. Around line 38, change "VIRTUAL_NOKIA 0" to "VIRTUAL_NOKIA 1".
	- 1 for real board
	- 0 for simulated board

3. Go to Project -> Options for target "..."

4. Under Debug tab, click radio button left of "Use: Stellaris ICDI".

5. Click OK.

6. Open TExaSdisplay.exe application.

7. In toolbar, click "open serial port" button.

8. Click View -> Nokia5110

9. Press Reset button on Tiva to start the game.

- If nothing shows up, try going to COM -> close and open next.
