1. Go to http://edx-org-utaustinx.s3.amazonaws.com/UT601x/download.html

2. Download TExaS for Labs 4-16 (don't need 2).

3. Execute the TExaS_Install.exe

4. Under “C:\Keil” or whatever directory you installed it to, 
   the starter files are found in Labware >> Lab15_VirtualSpaceInvaders.

5. Run SpaceInvaders.uvproj

6. Go to Project >> Options for Target ... 

7. In the Target tab, change the Operating System to TExaS.

8. In the Debug tab, toggle 'Use Simulator'.

9. Click OK.

To simulate the code:

a. Rebuild all target files.

b. Debug.

c. Go to Peripherals on the toolbar and open:
   - TExaS Nokia to simulate the LCD
   - TExaS ADC to simulate the switches and slide potentiometer.
	- Click 'Right' for SW2 and 'Left' for SW1.
